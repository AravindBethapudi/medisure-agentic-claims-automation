### **1.1 Purpose**

This manual describes MediSure Healthâ€™s standards for identifying, preventing, and investigating potential instances of Fraud, Waste, and Abuse (FWA) within the claims submission process.

It sets requirements for:

* Automated detection systems
* Manual investigation procedures
* Provider compliance expectations

---

### **1.2 Scope**

Applicable to:

* All claims submitted to MediSure Health
* All provider types
* All benefit plans
* All adjudication channels

---

### **1.3 Key Definitions**

**Fraud:**
Intentional deception or misrepresentation to obtain unauthorized benefits.

**Waste:**
Overuse of services or inefficient practices that increase costs.

**Abuse:**
Practices inconsistent with sound medical or financial standards.

---

### **1.4 Regulatory Framework**

This policy aligns with:

* CMS Program Integrity Manual
* HIPAA Fraud & Abuse Standards
* Federal False Claims Act
* State-level Medicaid regulations

* 0â€“1 days â†’ **Likely duplicate**
